//This script describes a chess game. 
////See Readme.md for conventions used in this script. 
//It stops after the first 2 moves.
//To simplify further the clock, board and pieces have not been represented by objects.
//The timeline objects ChessVisit1 to ChessVisit5 are punctuated by events in capital letters.
//Events have time stamps. The duration of a timeline is the difference between timestamps in preceding 
//and succeeding events.
//Time proceeds from left to right.
//The games1 object moves from left to right as property changes occur in the Events.

//The script could show pieces being taken as the game proceeds; it could show the results of the analysis of a chess engine,
//it could show changes in the facial expression of the players.
//No attempt has been made to represent the internal state of the players.

DisplaySOs *	//display all the objects.
//RandomWalk 30
NaturalText
QuerySOs RO_EVENT
RandomWalk 30
